Darion and Rodney's journey began in early 2019, navigating the challenging early stages of the trading world. They started with iMarketsLive, driven by an unrelenting curiosity to understand **what truly makes the markets move**. This burning question fueled their passion and propelled them into various trading communities. Their breakthrough came in the *Lions of Forex* group, where they rapidly honed their trading skills and comprehension. However, their growth quickly outpaced the resources available, prompting them to seek more advanced knowledge. After navigating through months of unproductive mentors and groups, they received an invitation from Steve Mauro to join *BTMM (Beat The Market Maker)*. There, their skills skyrocketed as they mastered advanced strategies and cultivated an ironclad trading psychology. Now, equipped with years of trading experience and expertise across multiple strategies, Darion and Rodney are launching a cutting-edge algorithmic service. Their goal is to empower everyday traders by addressing common concerns and making professional-grade tools accessible to everyone. We invite you to join us on this journey—let’s succeed together!
