Section 2: How Wormholes Might Work

  • By bending space and time, wormholes could offer a faster-than-light shortcut across the cosmos. This section explains how they theoretically reshape spacetime.
  • Additional Point: Wormholes could, in theory, connect not only locations in space but also different points in time, though this idea brings about complex scientific challenges.

Section 3: Types Of Wormholes

  • Traversable Wormholes: These theoretical tunnels could allow safe travel if they stay open long enough. However, they would need "exotic matter" to stabilize.
  • Non-Traversable Wormholes: Much like black holes, these wormholes would collapse too quickly for anything to pass through.
  • Additional Point: Some scientists believe that naturally occurring traversable wormholes could exist, but none have been observed to date
